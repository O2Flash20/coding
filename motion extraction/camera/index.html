Motion Extraction [Camera]

Inspired by this video (and the desire to cheat at video games by seeing players moving from far away), this my quick and easy solution to motion extraction in real time: by subtracting the current frame from one a few milliseconds before, all the pixels that stayed the same color in that time will go to black, letting the user see only what changed.

If the videos don't fit on your screen, you could do [CTRL -] or [CTRL +] until they do.
